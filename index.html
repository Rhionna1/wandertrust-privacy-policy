Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 7, 2026

WanderTrust ("we," "our," or "us,") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the WanderTrust mobile application ("the app"). Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

WanderTrust collects the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your first name, last name, username, email address, and a securely hashed version of your password.
  • Vacation and Travel Data: We collect information you voluntarily enter into the App, including vacation details, excursion schedules, travel party names, and photos you choose to attach to your vacations and excursions.
  • Device Permissions: The App may request access to your device's photo library to allow you to attach photos to your vacations and excursions. The App also requests permission to schedule notifications and exact alarms to deliver travel reminders.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely to provide the features and functionality of the WanderTrust application. Specifically, we use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and maintain your account session.
  • Store and display your vacation plans, excursion schedules, and travel party information.
  • Deliver scheduled notifications and alerts for your vacation and excursion dates.
  • Generate vacation summary reports within the application.

3. Data Storage

All data entered into WanderTrust is stored locally on your device using a SQLite database managed by the Android Room Framework. We do not transmit your personal data to any external servers, third-party services, or cloud databases. Your data remains on your device at all times.

4. Password Security

Your password is never stored in plain text. WanderTrust uses SHA-256 cryptographic hashing to securely store your password. This means that even if your device data were accessed, your original password could not be recovered.

5. Photos and Media

If you choose to attach photos to your vacations or excursions, WanderTrust accesses those photos directly from your device's local storage. Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared outside of your device. The App uses Android's persistent URI permission system to maintain access to selected photos across app sessions.

6. Notifications and Alarms

WanderTrust uses Android's AlarmManager to schedule local device notifications for vacation and excursion reminders. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

7. Third-Party Services

WanderTrust does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

8. Children's Privacy

WanderTrust is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
